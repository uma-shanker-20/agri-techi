Cows

Cows are domesticated bovine animals that are raised for their milk, meat, and hides. They are an important part of agriculture and are found all over the world.

Attribute Details
Price in Market 50000 - 150000
Diet Grass, Fruits, grains
Daily Food Intake 25 - 50 kg
Breeding Once a year
Pregnancy Period 9 months
Milk Production 20 - 30 liters per day

Buffalo

Buffalo are large bovine animals that are often used for their milk and meat. They are known for their strength and are commonly found in Asia and Africa.

Attribute Details
Price in Market 50000 - 200000
Diet Grass, Weeds, grains
Daily Food Intake 30 - 60 kg
Breeding Once every two years
Pregnancy Period 10 months
Milk Production 10 - 20 liters per day
